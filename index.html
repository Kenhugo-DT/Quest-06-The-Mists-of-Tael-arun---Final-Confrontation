Locations of Tael'arun

Whispering Forest

A mysterious forest where the leaves seem to whisper ancient secrets.
The trees are ancient and twisted, their branches creating intricate patterns against the misty sky.

Echoing Caves

Deep caverns where every sound resonates with mysterious echoes.
Strange crystals line the walls, glowing with an inner light.

Shimmering Riverside

A peaceful riverside where the water gleams with an otherworldly shine.
The waters are said to have healing properties.

Crumbling Tower

An ancient watchtower, now in ruins. Despite its deteriorated state,
magical energies still pulse through its old stones.

Dragon's Lair

A massive cavern atop the highest peak, home to the fearsome dragon of Tael'arun.
The entrance is scattered with charred remains and treasure fragments.
